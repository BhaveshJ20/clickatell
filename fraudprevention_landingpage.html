Clickatell’s solutions protect your business, and your customers, from:

E-commerce Fraud

E-commerce leaves your business vulnerable to scams such as order scalping, billing changes, the manipulation of sales volumes and fraudulent transactions.

Human Error and Reliability

Security systems remain vulnerable to human error and personnel fraud. Automated transactional solutions with real-time reporting and bespoke feedback mitigates against this.

Fraud Accounts Trading

Fraud accounts trading allows criminals to circumvent regular security measures and registration barriers such as CAPTCHA and email confirmation.

SIM Farms

To circumvent e-commerce verification processes, attackers build SIM farms, allowing them to engage in bulk account registration, PIN duplication and other forms of fraud.

Ability to help your customers across multiple chat channels, each with specific verification processes, mitigating against fraud.
