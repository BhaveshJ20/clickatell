Webinar presenters

Mark Walker Associate Vice President - South, East and West Africa at IDC.

Mark currently leads the sub-Saharan Africa research and consulting practice at IDC MEA. He has over 25 years of experience in the information technology and telecommunications industry. In his position at IDC, Walker is responsible for leading consulting, research, and analyses of technology markets across the Africa, along with analysis of the larger politico-socio-economic environments in which these markets exist. As a large proportion of research conducted by IDC MEA is of a primary nature, he has first-hand experience of the various technology markets in the region.

Pieter de Villiers Clickatell CEO and Co-Founder

Pieter is one of the youngest mobile industry veterans with over 14 years of experience in global telecoms, mobile messaging and mobile payments backed by the likes of Sequoia Capital. As the CEO and co-founder of Clickatell, a global leader in mobile communications and chat commerce, Pieter has successfully lead his team to grow a multi million dollar profitable business operating on multiple continents. Since its launch, Clickatell boasts one of the largest messaging-as-a-service footprints globally delivering services for large Fortune 500 brands as well more than 13 000 medium and small business customers and application developers across 224 countries and territories.

Kreegan Govindsamy Head of Product Management at Discovery Holdings

Kreegan currently leads the Product Management capability at Discovery Holdings, responsible for taking new digital products to market across the Group. In his role, Kreegan is responsible for the vision, adoption and overall success of one of the largest chat commerce ecosystems in Africa. He was directly involved in the Launch of Discovery Bank and has crafted the go-to-market strategy for product innovations spanning long and short-term insurance, medical insurance and investments.

Werner Lindemann Clickatell SVP - Commercial: Middle East & Southern Africa

With over two decades of experience in commercial strategy and business development at Fortune 50 and high-growth innovative technology and communications companies, Werner is responsible for executing Clickatell’s business growth strategy in the region. Lindemann has a proven track record of successfully developing innovative information technology and communications companies. Most recently he was Chief Sales Officer at BCX, Africa’s premier ICT solutions and service provider, and helped build Mediamark’s operations as Managing Director. He spent over a decade at IBM where he served as Vice President of Global Technology and was a Consultant at PwC.
