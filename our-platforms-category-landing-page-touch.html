Engage with your customers where they are: in chat

Give today’s mobile first customers the delightful, intuitive support they expect in the way they prefer: via their favorite chat apps. Clickatell Touch enables you to seamlessly incorporate mobile chat apps into your contact centers, allowing agents and chat bots to instantaneously respond to mobile chat-generated enquires from within existing web-based consoles and easily switch between different conversations and communication channels. What’s more, Touch integrates with your existing support, CRM, and enterprise systems for more informed customer experiences.

Flow

Create a rich self-service experience for your business—one that that can be written once and deployed across multiple mobile channels such as WhatsApp, SMS and USD.

Flow enables your company to:

  • More rapidly build customer self-service workflows that support a broad range of customer support, engagement, and transactional use cases
  • Allow business analysts with minimal to no development experience needed to rapidly develop powerful mobile workflows for mobile channels
  • Quickly optimize and streamline workflows to provide better customer experiences
  • Deliver a wide range of business responses – from general queries and frequently asked questions, to account information look-ups and convenience/utility workflows
  • Minimize costs and maximize return on customer-facing, self-service activities
