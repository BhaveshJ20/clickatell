What is SMS and why should your business use it?

SMS (commonly referred to as a text messaging) stands for Short Message Service which is a protocol used for sending short text messages via mobile networks. It’s one of the oldest and most commonly used forms of communication thanks to its universal nature – even the most basic cellphones can receive this form of text messaging.

Clickatell’s SMS gateway enables businesses to send bulk SMS messages to 6 billion mobile phone users across the globe – that’s over 85% of the world population. It comes as no surprise then, that one-way (1-Way) bulk SMS messaging is often a preferred marketing and communication channel for businesses, and not only due to its vast reach.

Here are a couple of reasons why businesses are choosing to use one-way SMS

No Lead time

SMS messages can be created and delivered to the recipient’s phone within a matter of minutes.

Low cost

SMS marketing costs significantly less than other advertising options such print, radio, or TV. Combined with the brilliant open rates, the low cost makes bulk SMS one of the best options for a maximum return on investment (ROI) that you’ll find.

Open rate

Bulk SMS is not only used for marketing purposes though. Many businesses use one-way SMS messaging to improve internal processes and communication between employees, for example, and financial institutions take advantage of the immediacy of SMS for time sensitive messages like one-time PINs.

Two-way text messaging

What is two-way SMS?

Research has shown that as much as 64% of customers want to be able to engage with companies via SMS services. Two-way (2-way) messaging enables businesses to do just that. Clickatell’s two-way messaging solutions work with a short code or long number that enables your customers to reply to your SMS messages allowing you to start meaningful conversations with them.

Here are just a few ways that you can use two-way text messaging to grow your business

Lead generation

SMS messages can be created and delivered to the recipient’s phone within a matter of minutes.

SMS surveys

Get valuable customer feedback with SMS surveys that allow your customers to rate products or services.

Improved customer support

Two-way text messaging is ideal for any organization offering a support service. SMS is uniquely personal, instantaneous, and enables the customer to receive exactly the help or support they’re looking for – no matter where they are.

Order tracking and delivery confirmations

Two-way messaging is the perfect way to enable customers to track their orders. By simply sending a text message with a tracking number to your short code, customers can receive a response with the latest status of their delivery.

What are my options?

Short codes

A short code is a memorable number between 3 and 7 digits in length. Short codes are ideal for SMS marketing campaigns and promotions where you want people to easily remember your number. Short codes are country specific and can't be used if you want to receive messages from international customers so you’ll need to have a short code in the country where your customers are.

Long numbers

Two-way long numbers, like the name implies, are long telephone numbers which can be up to 16 digits in length but are usually the length of a standard local phone number. Long numbers are cheaper to use than short codes and allow you to receive replies from local and selected international destinations. If you need to receive replies to your text messages but it’s not as important that people easily remember your number, then a long number is the most cost-effective solution to start a two-way conversation with your customers.
