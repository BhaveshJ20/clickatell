Chat Commerce brought to life

Clickatell's Chat 2 Pay lets you deliver a new way to accept payments from your customers, by using chat messaging rather than having them disclose credit card details over the phone or having to use a point-of-sale device.

How does Chat 2 Pay work?

  • 1

    A payment request is triggered by the call center agent from the order management system (OMS) and sent to the customer as a link.

  • 2

    The customer receives the secure link via SMS or WhatsApp. Clicking the link directs them to a fully-hosted, responsive checkout page.

  • 3

    The customer fills in their payment details, and submits payment, receiving confirmation of their order and a receipt, also via chat.
