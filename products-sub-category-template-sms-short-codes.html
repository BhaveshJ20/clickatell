Do you need short codes or long numbers?

Short codes—5-digit or 6-digit numbers—are best for high-volume messaging, when you want to quickly reach a high volume of customers. Long numbers—10-digit phone numbers with area codes—are better for more personalized 1:1 communications, and for offering a consistent way to reach your business via phone and SMS.
