The real cost of unmitigated chargebacks

Revenue Lost

Traditional chargeback resolution is an involved and resource-consuming process, needing time and effort from costly, trained and reliable staff. Over and above process costs, your business could lose up to 40% of its revenue through chargebacks and associated fraud.

Resolution Costs

Businesses evaluating their chargeback losses often fail to factor resource allocation, transactional fees, process expenses and chargeback fees. The bottom-line cost of an individual chargeback is often 2.5 times the actual sale price.

Reputational Damage

Fraud chargeback or dispute resolution leads to negative perceptions of your business and an immediate and significant loss of consumer trust and increase in customer friction. With higher insurance costs, processing and payment fees and account management fees, unmitigated chargebacks have the potential to hinder your business’ reputation.
