Strengthening the relationships between brands and their customers

Innovative mobile experiences empower brands to connect, engage, and transact with customers. We make those experiences possible.

For more than 19 years, Clickatell has been a global technology company and innovator in mobile messaging, engagement, and commerce.

Today, we’re powering the chat economy with smart solutions that bring brands easily into the chat apps their customers use and love.

Mobile consumers can now interact with business on demand, at their convenience, using the channel of their choice such as SMS, WhatsApp, and Facebook Messenger.

We cover more than 220 territories, more than 1000 networks, and 85 percent of the world’s population, with an SMS reach of more than 5 billion users.

A big idea. A tiny apartment.

In a tiny apartment in Cape Town, our (now) CEO Pieter de Villiers, his twin brother Casper, and two friends Danie du Toit and Patrick Lawson embarked on creating an application programming interface (API) between cellphones and computers. They did it! They create a world’s first: four lines of code that created a way to send SMS from the internet to cell phones.

The team then introduced Clickatell’s chat capabilities in developing countries, including South Africa. Here, limited transportation infrastructure makes it difficult to shop and bank at stores and financial institutions, and a cell phone is the primary communication device. Brands were in search of a mobile messaging, engagement and commerce solution to conduct business with these mobile-based customers. We delivered.

Today, Clickatell’s solutions are utilized by companies around the world and across industries for customer care, commerce, customer acquisition, customer retention, and customer expansion.

Pieter de Villiers

CEO

Pieter is a South African born, Silicon Valley experienced entrepreneur and angel investor with a passion for mobile technology and the empowerment it leads to. Pieter is also one of the youngest mobile industry veterans with over 17 years’ experience in global telecoms, mobile messaging, and mobile payments. He is the first South African to be backed by top-tier Silicon Valley VC firm Sequoia Capital and has spent nearly 10 years in San Francisco where he successfully grew Clickatell into a multi-million dollar company operating across multiple continents. Since its launch, Clickatell boasts one of the largest messaging-as-a-service footprints globally as well as one of the fastest growing mobile payment networks, serving thousands of businesses across more than 220 countries and territories.

Danie du Toit

One of the four original founders of Clickatell, Danie was the custodian of our brand and product offering until 2005. Sadly, after a long battle with leukemia, Danie passed away on August 16th, 2005.

We recognize his vision and contribution in establishing Clickatell as one of the leading mobile enablers in the world, we continue to track the 'footprints' Danie left in the company that are inspiring till this day.

Goodbye good friend!
